About Me

I am a 3D Level Artist and World Builder with over 5 years of experience in content creation. My passion lies in crafting atmospheric, immersive environments that tell a story through every detail.

Currently based in Vienna (Open to Relocation), I specialize in the full cycle of environment production in Unreal Engine 5 — from initial blockout to final cinematic lighting and optimization.

Core Expertise:

  • Full-cycle Environment Production in UE5
  • High-end Technical Optimization (Lumen, Nanite)
  • Advanced Cinematic Lighting & Post-processing
  • Environmental Storytelling & LookDev
  • Custom 3D Asset Creation in Blender
